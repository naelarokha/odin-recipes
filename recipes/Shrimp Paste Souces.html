How to cook Fresh Tomato Shrimp Paste Sauce

Sambal is a staple in every Indonesian dish, especially fried and grilled dishes. Eating without it will likely dampen your appetite. However, with it, your meal will be more satisfying and fulfilling. Without any further ado, Let's learn how.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 finger-length piece of grilled shrimp paste
  • 1 ounce of cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 cloves of shallots
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp seasoning
  • ½ tsp finely grated brown sugar

Let's Cook

  1. Grind the cayenne pepper, garlic, and shallots together.
  2. Add the tomatoes to the spice mortar and pestle, grind again until smooth, then stir well.
  3. Add salt, seasoning, finely grated brown sugar, and the previously roasted shrimp paste.
  4. Blend all ingredients until thoroughly combined.
  5. Adjust the seasoning, adding salt or sugar until you achieve the desired flavor.
  6. The shrimp paste chili sauce with fresh tomatoes is ready to be served with grilled fish, fried chicken, or braised tempeh.

A very easy recipe, isn't it? Give this recipe a try, and we hope your family enjoys it.
