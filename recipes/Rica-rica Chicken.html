How To Cook Rica-rica Chicken

Rica-Rica Chicken is a Manadonese specialty known for its spicy and savory flavor. Here's a simple recipe for making delicious Rica-Rica Chicken at home.

Ingredients:

Main Ingredients

  • Whole chicken (about 1 kg), cut into pieces
  • Tablespoons lime juice
  • Teaspoon salt
  • A little oil for frying and sautéing

Ground Spices

  • 10 Shallots
  • 10-20 curly red chilies (to taste)
  • 5-10 Red's bird eye chilies (to taste)
  • 2 Pieces ginger
  • 1-2 Pieces of turmeric
  • 3-4 Candlenuts (optional)

Aromatic Seasonings and Toppings:

  • 2 Stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • 3 Bay leaves (optional)
  • 5 Kaffir lime leaves, veins removed
  • 1 Stalk spring onion, chopped
  • 1 Bunch basil leaves, plucked and washed
  • Salt, sugar, and chicken stock powder to taste
  • water to taste

How To Make:

  1. Marinate Chicken: Rub the chicken pieces with lime juice and salt. Let stand for at least 15-20 minutes to remove the fishy smell and allow the spices to absorb.
  2. Fry Chicken: Heat oil, fry the chicken until half-cooked or slightly browned, remove, and drain.
  3. Prepare the Seasoning: Blend all the ground spices using a blender or pestle.
  4. Sauté the Seasoning: Heat a little oil in a pan. Sauté the ground spices with lemongrass, bay leaves, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal (if using) until fragrant and cooked through.
  5. Cook the Chicken: Add the fried chicken to the sautéed spices. Stir well until the chicken is coated with the spices.
  6. Add Water and Seasoning: Add enough water (about 3/4 cup). Add salt, sugar, and chicken stock powder. Cook over medium heat until the spices are absorbed and the sauce has reduced/thickened.
  7. Finishing Touches: Add the sliced ​​spring onions and basil leaves. Stir well and cook briefly until the leaves wilt. Adjust the seasoning.
  8. Serve: Rica-rica chicken is ready to be served warm with white rice.
